<br>Trees slumber.
<br>Leaves dress the drive.
<br>Damp and dank,
<br>Tannins leach brown.
<br>Stretch and rake,
<br>Rake and gather.
<br>Piles grow.
<br>Slow……slow……
<br>The next season,
<br>Settling in your heart.
<br>Satisfaction in effort,
<br>That rooks applaud.
